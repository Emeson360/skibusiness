Product Brief

Nigeria is ranked the 2nd largest sorghum producing country in the world as at 2017 with an annual production quantity of 6.94 million metric tons. Our sorghum comes in different colour varieties (white, red and orange), and our processing facility currently has a capacity of processing a minimum of 150mt daily.

Sorghum

Sorghum is one of the top five cereal crops in the world , its origin is traced to Northeastern Africa, It is truly a versatile crop that can be grown as a grain or forage.  It has multiple usage which spans across the production of Malt, Beer, Beer powder, Sorghum meal and Livestock feed among others.

It is referred to as a high-energy, drought-tolerant crop that is environmentally friendly.
